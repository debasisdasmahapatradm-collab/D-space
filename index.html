üçΩÔ∏è Modular Kitchen Solutions

Our modular kitchens are designed for the modern Indian home, focusing on efficient space utilization, durability, and a flawless aesthetic. Choose from a variety of finishes to match your style.

Laminate Kitchen

The most popular choice for its durability and cost-effectiveness. Laminate finishes are highly resistant to scratches and moisture, offering a wide array of colors and textures from matte to high-gloss. Ideal for high-traffic kitchens.

Acrylic Kitchen

Known for its luxurious, mirror-like high-gloss finish, an Acrylic kitchen makes the space look brighter and more expansive. It offers excellent color retention and a premium look, perfect for a sleek, contemporary design.

PU Kitchen

Polyurethane (PU) finish is a premium, smooth, and matte/satin finish applied over MDF or HDF. It provides a seamless, sophisticated look and is highly durable against heat and moisture. Perfect for designer kitchens seeking a European feel.

Modular Laminate Finish

Built-in factory with precision and standard sizing, featuring durable laminate finish. Offers quick installation, perfect finish consistency, and can be disassembled if you move homes.

Modular Acrylic Finish

Factory-made unit with a high-gloss, reflective acrylic surface. Provides a premium, sleek aesthetic with excellent scratch resistance and a modern look. Available in a variety of vibrant colours.

Carpentry Laminate Finish

On-site construction by skilled carpenters, offering complete customisation to any irregular space. Finished with laminate for durability and choice of texture/colour.
